Tortilla Soup

Description

This tortilla soup recipe is a spicy throwback to a holiday party/exchange that I participated in during the pandemic. It achieves a two out of five stars for spiciness and five out of five for deliciousness. üî•üî•

Ingredients üç±

  • 4 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 2 chicken boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 T commercial chili powder
  • 1 T dried onion flakes
  • 6 oz can of tomato paste (must be paste, not sauce)
  • 2 T Tabasco chipotle sauce
  • 5 T hot sauce, for heat (Chalula recommended)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 2 medium avocados
  • 1 bag of tortilla chips

Prepare ü•ò

  1. Dissolve the bullion cubes in 4 cups of boiling water.
  2. Place the chicken in the boiling chicken broth until cooked.
  3. Remove the chicken, allow to cool, and tear into pieces.
  4. Add the chili powder, onion, tomato paste, chipotle sauce, hot sauce, and lime juice to the broth and simmer and stir.
  5. Test for saltiness and salt to taste.
  6. To assemble, place broken tortilla chips, diced avocado, and chicken pieces in a bowl and pour broth mixture over them.

Note: Add hot sauce to taste.
