Dead Simple Chili

Description

A recipe dictated from memory coming from my SO's mother. She described it as "dead simple."

Ingredients üç±

  • 2 lbs. stew meat
  • 1 pk chili seasoning
  • 8 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 1 c beef broth + "a small amount to mix with corn starch"
  • 1 T corn starch

Prepare (Steps) ü•ò

  • Mix ingredients in crock pot
  • Low heat for 8 hours or high heat for 4-6 hours
  • Mix 1 T corn starch with a small amount of beef broth
  • Add to crock pot 30 minutes before serving
